Any brand, creator, or influencer looking to grow, monetize, or promote products can register and get started.

How do creators earn money?

Creators earn commissions by sharing products within their community and driving real sales through their unique shop pages.

How is Grofeed different from other platforms?

Grofeed focuses on ROI-driven influencer marketing with real engagement, lower return rates, and data transparency for both brands and creators.

Is there any cost to join Grofeed?

Joining Grofeed is free for creators. Brands may choose from flexible pricing options based on their campaign needs.

How do creators receive payouts?

Creators receive their earnings directly to their linked bank accounts or preferred payment method. Payouts are processed weekly or monthly, based on completed sales and the platform’s payout cycle. A detailed earnings report is available in your dashboard at all times.
